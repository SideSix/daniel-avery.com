Hi, my name is Dan. I create stuff.

One day back in the 1970's, I realized that I liked creating stuff. It's a fascinating process, creation, and I've spent a lot of my adult life engaged in and thinking about the process.

Of course when you start talking about what really happens in the creative process you immediately sound "crazy as a loon" to those listening, unless your Michelangelo.

{% blockquote Michelangelo, Famous Artist %} I just chiseled away the marble that wasn't David. {% endblockquote %}

I guess if you are actually Michelangelo, the women come and go talking about you. (Sorry. That's an old English major's joke.)
